CONTEXT

For food enthusiasts, sometimes we like to go back to excellent addresses that we have tried before : you don't change a winning team ! We don't want a new restaurant address, we want one we already know and loved! But it was so long ago...we don't remember the name of the restaurant...and this one, we already went there, but we don't know if the food was good, how can we remember? For example, when someone comes to visit us and we would like to take him/her to that great restaurant we went to a long time ago...what's its name again? Or our friend would prefer Japanese food, where can I take him to eat something of sure value? No question of taking risks. Sometimes we don't want to rely on the opinions of restaurant websites, we prefer to rely on our own experiences: we trust our tastes! How can we remember those good restaurants we went to in the past, and how can we not go back to those we didn't like if we don't remember?

SOLUTION

Why not create your own restoration guide? We can only trust the address when it comes to our own opinions! There are so many cities where we have gone, and so many restaurants that we don't remember the name and address when we went to Italy, or when we went to Paris, or even restaurants in our city where we forget to go when we look for somewhere to eat. With a database system, it would then be possible to note down our own personal notes, on our opinion about a restaurant where we have just eaten. We could then easily access this information on a website, from any device. It would be interesting to classify the restaurants among those we liked and hated, or by specialty: an Italian, Chinese, Japanese, French, Spanish, Breton, ... restaurant. All that will be left to do is to create a "search" section in the site, where you can find certain restaurants in the database according to their location or the type of cuisine for example.
